Cheese Cake Recipe

There's nothing quite like a rich, velvety cheesecake to satisfy my sweet tooth cravings. And when you add a bright lemon twist with a vibrant blueberry sauce? Well, that's just cheesecake perfection in my book! I've been baking up cheesecakes for years, but this lemon blueberry version is definitely a new favorite. Let me walk you through all the deliciousness.

"Life is short. Eat dessert first."
- Jacques Torres

Ingredients

  • 1 9-inch graham cracker crust
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • Zest of 1 large lemon
  • 4 8-ounce packages cream cheese (room temperature)
  • 4 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • Homemade blueberry sauce

How to Cook !

Here you can find how to start cooking it!

It starts with that buttery graham cracker crust. You can't beat the classic! I like to make my own by pulsing graham crackers into crumbs and mixing with melted butter and sugar. So simple, yet such an important base for our creamy filling.
Speaking of the filling, this is where we really bring that lemon zing. Cream cheese is the star, of course, but the addition of fresh lemon zest makes it taste so vibrant and refreshing. I always make sure my cream cheese is at room temperature for ultra smooth incorporation.
Once the cream cheese is beaten with the sugar until light and fluffy, I mix in the eggs one at a time, then the vanilla and heavy cream. That cream makes the texture even more luxurious.
After pouring the filling into the crust, it's time for a water bath. This helps the cheesecake bake gently and evenly without cracking on top. I always cross my fingers it works!
While the cheesecake cools, I whip up a quick blueberry sauce on the stovetop with fresh or frozen blueberries, sugar, lemon juice and a touch of cornstarch to thicken it up. This vibrant purple sauce is the perfect tangy-sweet complement to the rich, creamy lemon cheesecake.
Once the cheesecake is completely cooled and chilled, I slice it up and drizzle each piece with that gorgeous blueberry sauce. The flavors and textures are simply divine together!
This lemon blueberry cheesecake definitely requires a bit of effort, but to me it's so worth it for that perfect sweet-tart creamy indulgence. Baking is my love language, and this is one dessert I'm always happy to make and share.

If you try this recipe, let me know what you think! As always, happy baking!
